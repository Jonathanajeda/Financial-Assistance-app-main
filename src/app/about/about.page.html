Welcome to the Department of Social Welfare and Development San Luis Batangas

The town of San Luis, formerly called “Balibago,” was among the southern barangays of old Taal. It was named Balibago because of its proximity to Balibago River, which separates San Luis from Taal. The river was so called because of its ever-changing course, which is vernacular means “pabago-bago.”
